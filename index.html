ACWM-Phys: Investigating Generalized Physical Interaction
in Action-Conditioned Video World Models

Anonymous Authors  ·  NeurIPS 2026 Submission
Video supplement — all videos show Ground Truth (left) | Prediction (right)
We introduce ACWM-Phys, a benchmark spanning four physically diverse interaction regimes (rigid-body, deformable, particle, kinematics), and ACWM-DiT, a latent diffusion transformer baseline trained with flow matching. Videos supplement the PDF with temporal predictions that cannot be conveyed in static figures.
